Automated Auction House sniper for Forza Horizon 6. Watches for the car you set up, buys it out the instant it appears, collects it, and loops. ~10% buyout rate, generally snipes a car in under 5 minutes.

### What's new:

- **Settings panel inside the overlay.** New Settings tab next to Status; edit match sensitivity, loop speed, auto-stop limits, notifications, hotkeys, HDR mode, moving background, and overlay visibility in screenshots / recordings, without touching config.json. Changes apply live.
- **Wider resolution & monitor support.** 1080p, 1440p and 4K all work directly. Ultrawide, 16:10 and 4:3 setups: run FH6 windowed at 1920×1080 and the black bars get cropped automatically.
- **HDR support.** HDR was shifting FH6's lime UI toward yellow and breaking color detection. Fixed. Extra HDR mode toggle in Settings for displays that shift even more.
- **Slow-load fix.** Was reporting "no cars" when the Auction House just hadn't finished loading yet. Now recognizes the loading screen and waits.
- **Polish.** Filled checkboxes (the old ones looked janky), tabbed Status / Settings layout, collapsible Settings sections that scroll on short screens, faster captures at high resolutions, cleaner state names in the log file.

### Quick start

1. Download **FH6-Sniper.zip** below and extract anywhere.
2. In FH6, set up your search (Make / Model / Max Buyout) and back out to the **Search config** screen.
3. Double-click **FH6-Sniper.exe**, click back into the game, press **F8** to start.

Full setup, hotkeys, and config options in the [README](https://github.com/FrostyIsBored/FH6-Auction-House-Sniper#readme).

### Requirements

- Windows 10/11
- Forza Horizon 6 on PC
- 16:9 resolution at 1080p, 1440p or 4K. Ultrawide / 16:10 / 4:3 users: run windowed at 1920×1080.
- Very Low graphics preset
- Wired ethernet strongly recommended

### Heads-up

Windows SmartScreen will warn you because the exe is unsigned: click **More info** > **Run anyway**.
